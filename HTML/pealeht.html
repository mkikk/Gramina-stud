Gramina is a family run stud, located in Tartu, Estonia. We are dedicated to reproduction, training, competing and selling of sporthorses. We thrive towards the highest quality in everything.

Gramina stud was founded in 2018 when we started to build our first stable. Half year after starting, we were able to bring in our first horses. Since then, the growth of own horses and clients has been significantly rising. We breed 2 to 4 mares every year and use elite stallions only. We are currently building our foundation in breeding and are very satisfied with the results.
